Challenger 300/350

Memory Items

Version 1.1 - UNOFFICIAL and
FOR TRAINING ONLY

Instructions

CABIN ALTITUDE

  1. Crew O2 Masks DON
  2. Pilot & Copilot O2MASK/NORM O2 MASK
  3. Thrust Levers IDLE
  4. Autopilot Disconnect
  5. FLIGHT SPOILERS EMER
  6. Descend at MMO / VMO but not below min safe alt.

L(R) BLEED LEAK

  1. XBLEED Closed
  2. Affected BLEED OFF

    3. If RIGHT BLEED LEAK is on:

  3. L BLEED On
  4. APU BLEED OFF

PACK LEAK

  1. AIR SOURCE TRIM AIR ONLY

L(R) PYLON BLEED LEAK

  1. Thrust Lever IDLE

TRIM AIR LEAK

  1. AIR SOURCE PACK AIR ONLY

APU FIRE

  1. APU FIRE FIRE Push
  2. FIRE EXT 2 ARMED Push

APU OVERTEMP

  1. APU OFF

TAWS Aurals/PULL UP

  1. Autopilot Disengage
  2. Thrust Levers APR
  3. FLIGHT SPOILERS RETRACT
  4. Climb at maximum practical rate
  5. LANDING GEAR UP

WINDSHEAR

  1. Autopilot Disengage
  2. Thrust Levers APR
  3. FLIGHT SPOILERS RETRACT
  4. Rotate smoothly to Takeoff/Go-around pitch attitude, allowing airspeed to decrease if necessary. Maintain wings level. Do not retract flaps or landing gear.

L(R) BATT OVERHEAT

  1. Affected BATT Switch OFF

Emergency Evacuation

  1. PARK/EMER BRAKE Set
  2. EMER DEPRESS ON
  3. L and R Eng & APU FIRE Push
  4. Evacuation Command
  5. Controlling Agency Notify
  6. STBY INST OFF
  7. L and R BATT OFF

Pitch Axis Uncommanded

  1. Master Disconnect Switch (MSW) Depress and Hold

Roll/Yaw Axis Uncommanded

  1. Master Disconnect Switch (MSW) Depress and Hold

Pitch Control Jam

  1. PITCH DISC PULL & TURN
  2. Determine the side without the jam and transfer control to that pilot.
  1. Do not apply additional control force to a jammed control.
  2. Do not reconnect.

Roll Control Jam

  1. ROLL DISC Lever PULL
  2. Determine the side without the jam and transfer control to that pilot.

WING ANTI-ICE LEAK

  1. WING ANTI-ICE Off

L(R) WING OVERHEAT

  1. WING ANTI-ICE Off

GEAR BAY OVERHEAT

  1. AIRSPEED 250 KIAS Max
  2. LANDING GEAR DOWN

NORM BRAKES FAIL

  1. Normal actual landing dist Multiply by:

    1.65 without TR

    1.50 with TR

  2. FLIGHT SPOILERS EMER
  3. PARK/EMER BRAKE Pull Smoothly

L(R) ENGINE EXCEEDANCE

  1. Thrust Lever Retard

Overspeed

  1. Thrust Levers IDLE
  2. Autopilot Disconnect
  3. FLIGHT SPOILERS EMER
  4. Level wings, then pitch up

L(R) ENGINE FIRE below V1

  1. Thrust Levers IDLE
  2. Brakes Maximum, or as required
  3. Thrust Reversers MAX REV, or as required

    4. After Stopping

  4. PARK/EMER BRAKE Set
  5. Affected ENG FIRE FIRE Push
  6. FIRE EXT 1 AND 2 ARMED Push
  7. EMER DEPRESS ON
  8. Non-affected ENG and APU FIRE Push
  9. Evacuation Command
  10. Controlling Agency Notify
  11. STBY INST OFF
  12. L and R BATT OFF

L(R) ENGINE FIRE above V1

  1. Rotate at VR
  2. Pitch Attitude Adjust to V2
  3. LANDING GEAR UP
  4. Affected Thrust Lever IDLE (Unless critical)
  5. Affected ENG FIRE FIRE Push

    6. If fire indications persist after 10 secs:

  6. FIRE EXT 1 ARMED Push

    7. If FIRE indications persist after 30 secs:

  7. FIRE EXT 2 ARMED Push

L(R) ENGINE FIRE in flight

  1. Affected Thrust Lever IDLE (Unless critical)
  2. Affected ENG FIRE FIRE Push

    3. If fire indications persist after 10 secs:

  3. FIRE EXT 1 ARMED Push

    4. If FIRE indications persist after 30 secs:

  4. FIRE EXT 2 ARMED Push

L(R) REVERSER UNSAFE below V1

  1. Thrust Levers IDLE
  2. Brakes Maximum, or as required
  3. Thrust Reversers MAX REV, or as required

L(R) REVERSER UNSAFE above V1

  1. Rotate at VR
  2. Pitch Attitude Adjust to V2
  3. LANDING GEAR UP after positive rate of climb
  4. Affected Thrust Lever IDLE

Dual Engine Flameout

  1. Crew O2 Masks(if above 14,000ft) DON
  2. O2 Mask/NORM O2 MASK
  3. Airspeed 185 KIAS

Engine Failure below V1

  1. Thrust Levers IDLE
  2. Brakes Maximum, or as required
  3. Thrust Reversers MAX REV, or as required

Engine Failure above V1

  1. Rotate at VR
  2. Pitch Attitude Adjust to V2
  3. LANDING GEAR UP after postive rate of climb

Rejected Takeoff

  1. Thrust Levers IDLE
  2. Brakes Maximum, or as required
  3. Thrust Reversers MAX REV, or as required

Cabin/Cockpit Fire, Smoke, Fumes

  1. Crew O2 DON, SELECT EMERGENCY
  2. Smoke Goggles DON
  3. O2 MASK/NORM O2 MASK

STALL

  1. Lower pitch attitude to reduce the angle-of-attack
  2. Thrust Levers APR
  3. FLIGHT SPOILERS RETRACT
  4. Level the wings

Unreliable Airspeed

CAUTION: Respect stall warning and stick pusher

  1. Autopilot Off
  2. Flight Directors Off

    3. Above MSA:

  3. Proceed to AVNC 02-17, step (7)

    4. Below MSA:

  4. Pitch / N1 15 degrees / TO
  5. Go-Around Initiate, clean up and climb above MSA

    6. In mountainous terrain / high MSA, above 15,000ft:

  6. Pitch / N1 10 degrees / CLB
